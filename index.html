how i repair phones.

What i will usualy do i get a request for a certain device (eg: One plus 3t). I will the quickly look up the prices for the replacement parts and tell the customer how much it would cost, then i will look at a disasembly video and see how dificult it will be to take apart and replace the broken part. then i wait and wait and wait for the parts to arrive (it take a long time because the parts usually come from china). After that i will turn the phone on and test if everything still works , then ill put the phone back together and mess with it for a bit (now that i can ) and ill give it back the next time i meet the customer (thats why i dont send phones out/take phones in).
