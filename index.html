Welcome!

Hi! This website was a piece of work set for ICT, the objective was to create a webpage not just a website this means having multiple pages that you could switch between. There was virtually only one rule, and that was that our webpage had to be related to it/ tech. At the time of writing this I had just recently swapped my sister’s old iPhone 4s for an iPod 6th gen that had a bad battery and a bad screen so what I did was ordered the appropriate parts from amazon and put it al together! At this approximate time, we had to make this site, so I decided to make a “business” page (hopefully it brings great things in the future!).

How much does it cost?

I try to keep prices as low as possible so that there is as little mark-up so the part that you order would normally be the cost of the repair! (This doesn’t mean that I won’t ask for tips, I still want to earn money from this) but I will demand more if I get a request in a certain timespan where I would be sacrificing my free time.

how i repair phones.

What I do to fix phones: 1. I’ll look up the device that I have been asked to fix (e.g.: iPhone 6), ill check how to safely take apart the phone and ill check if I have the tools for the job. 2. Ill go online and look for the parts price and availability, once I have done this I send a link to the best deal and make the customer order the part (that is my rule). 3. Wait…. 4. Yay! The parts have arrived, so I go to my room get out my tools and get to work! Every device is different, so I can’t say exactly what I do so if you are interested you can go follow me on Instagram and wait for me to stream repairs!
